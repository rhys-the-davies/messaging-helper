## Example Brand Voice (Delete this section after customizing)

Here's an example to inspire your own guidelines:

**Our Personality:**
- **Clear and Direct**: We value clarity over cleverness
- **Helpful**: We're here to make your job easier
- **Human**: We write like we talk—conversational but professional

**Voice Principles:**

1. **Simple, Not Simplistic**
   - Use everyday language
   - Avoid jargon unless necessary
   - When technical terms are needed, explain them
   - ✅ "Track your projects in one place"
   - ❌ "Leverage our unified project management paradigm"

2. **Confident, Not Arrogant**
   - Be decisive: "This helps you..." not "This might help..."
   - Show expertise without showing off
   - ✅ "We've helped 10,000 teams ship faster"
   - ❌ "We're the world's most innovative platform"

**Words We Love:**
- Simple, clear, fast, easy
- Help, build, create, ship
- You, your, we

**Words We Avoid:**
- Leverage, synergy, revolutionize
- Enterprise-grade (unless truly enterprise context)
- Game-changing, paradigm shift
