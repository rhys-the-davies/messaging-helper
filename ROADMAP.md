### Notion Integration
**What:** Sync your brand guidelines directly from Notion pages
**Why:** Many teams already keep their brand guidelines in Notion. Instead of copying content to markdown files, just point the app to your Notion workspace and it automatically pulls your latest guidelines.

**Benefits:**
- ‚úÖ No more manual copying between Notion and markdown files
- ‚úÖ Guidelines stay in sync automatically
- ‚úÖ Marketing team updates Notion, AI instantly knows
- ‚úÖ Single source of truth for brand guidelines

**Status:** Planned
**Complexity:** Medium
**Details:** [See GitHub Issue #1](../../issues/1) for implementation discussion

## üéØ Long-Term Vision (12+ Months)

**The goal:** Make this the single source of truth for brand-consistent content creation across your entire organization.

**We imagine:**
- Every team member using this daily
- Marketing, sales, support, product all aligned
- Zero off-brand content getting published
- New hires getting up to speed on brand voice in minutes
- Real-time brand guideline updates flowing to everyone
- Content quality that scales with team size

**How we'll get there:**
- Deep integrations with tools teams already use
- Smart automation that reduces manual work
- Analytics that show brand consistency trends
- Community-driven features that solve real problems

## ü§î How We Prioritize

Features move up the roadmap based on:

1. **User feedback** - What are people actually asking for?
2. **Impact** - Will this help most users significantly?
3. **Complexity** - Can we ship it without over-engineering?
4. **Vision alignment** - Does it move us toward our long-term goal?
5. **Maintainability** - Can we support it long-term?
