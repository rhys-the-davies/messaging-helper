## Example Messaging Guide (Delete this section after customizing)

Here's an example to inspire your own guidelines:

**Our Mission:**
Make project management simple and delightful.

**Our Value Propositions:**

1. **See Everything at a Glance**: No more hunting through tabs and tools. All your projects, tasks, and progress in one beautiful view.

2. **Collaborate Without the Chaos**: Comment, share, and update in real-time. Everyone stays in sync without endless meetings.

3. **Flexible for Any Team**: Whether you're building software, planning events, or managing content, adapt our tools to your workflow.

**Elevator Pitch:**
[Company] helps teams coordinate work without the chaos. See all your projects at a glance, collaborate in real-time, and adapt the tool to match how you work.

**Target Audiences:**

**Product Managers:**
- Who they are: Coordinating multiple teams and projects, need visibility
- How to speak to them: Focus on organization, clarity, keeping teams aligned
- Example: "See the status of every project in one view. Spot blockers before they become problems."

**Developers:**
- Who they are: Want to focus on building, dislike project management overhead
- How to speak to them: Focus on simplicity, integration with their tools, less context switching
- Example: "Update your tasks without leaving GitHub. Everything syncs automatically."

**Key Messages by Scenario:**

**Introducing Your Company:**
"We're [Company]. We make project management simple. See everything at a glance, collaborate without the chaos, and adapt to how your team works."

**Explaining Your Product:**
"It's a visual workspace where teams plan, track, and coordinate their work. Think of it as a dashboard for all your projectsâ€”you can see who's working on what, what's done, and what's coming up."
